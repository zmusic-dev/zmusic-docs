### Why can't I play music from Bilibili?

Music from Bilibili requires VIP users to play. If you are a VIP user, please check if your configuration is correct.

### What features does ZMusic VIP have?

ZMusic VIP is a subscription service that provides users with more features and services. Currently, VIP users can enjoy:

- Audio transcoding service, supporting Bilibili music playback

### My server has no network, can I use ZMusic?

ZMusic requires a network connection to work properly. If your server has no network, you cannot use ZMusic.

### Why does Netease Cloud Music only play for 30 seconds?

You may be playing a trial version of the music, which only plays for 30 seconds.

To play the full version, you need to log in to your Netease Cloud Music account and activate VIP service.

### How to log in to Netease Cloud Music account?

Please use the `/zm login` command to log in to your Netease Cloud Music account.
